Why Fabio exists

We got tired of ten different shops for one school list.

Fabio started with a simple annoyance: a school supply list has fifteen items on it, and buying them meant fifteen small trips — the stationery shop for notebooks, another for the geometry box, a third that happened to stock the right pencil box.

So we packed it all into one kit. Every notebook is ruled the way schools expect, every geometry box has all its tools, and every kit is checked by hand before it's sealed. If something's missing, it's on us to fix — not on a parent to notice at 7am.
